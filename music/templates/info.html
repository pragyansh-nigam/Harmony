How do I create a playlist ?

Playlists are a great way to save collections of music for your own listening and to share.
To create one :
1. Tap Your Library.
2. Tap CREATE.
3. Give your playlist a name.
4. Start adding songs (and we’ll help you along).

Is it only possible to shuffle play music ?

Any playlist with the Shuffle icon can be listened to on Shuffle.
Playlist without the Shuffle icon are available to Pick and Play. These are playlists you can play in any order and skip tracks as much as you like.

Welcome to Harmony for Artists! We’ve created this guide to walk you through setting up your artist profile, customizing your presence, and growing your fanbase on Harmony.

Can’t find what you’re looking for? Contact us and share your queries with us. Leave us a message addressing your issue regarding anything on our music app. We are ready to help you!

Harmony is an open platform for artistic expression. We believe in everyone’s right to share their voice. So generally, all creators and content are welcome on Harmony.
